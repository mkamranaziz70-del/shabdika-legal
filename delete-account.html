Delete your account

App: Shabdika  •  Package: com.shabdika.app  •  Developer: Muhammad Kamran

This page explains how to delete your Shabdika account, what data is removed, and what we keep.

Option 1 — Delete from inside the app

  1. Open Shabdika on your Android device.
  2. Sign in with the account you want to delete.
  3. Tap the Profile tab (bottom-right).
  4. Tap the Settings icon (top-right of the profile screen).
  5. Tap Delete Account.
  6. Confirm by entering your password and tapping Delete permanently.

Option 2 — Request deletion by email

If you cannot access the in-app option (for example, you have lost access to your account):

  1. Email shabkda@gmail.com from the email address registered to your Shabdika account.
  2. Use the subject line "Delete my Shabdika account".
  3. Include your registered email address in the body of the message so we can verify ownership.

We will action the request within 30 days and email you a confirmation when the deletion is complete.

Important: Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. If you are temporarily unable to access your account, consider signing out instead — you can return any time without losing your data.

Request partial data deletion only

If you want to delete some data but keep your account active — for example, removing a specific post or your profile picture — you can do that from inside the app:

  • Posts: open the post → tap the three-dot menu → Delete.
  • Comments: long-press the comment → Delete.
  • Profile picture: Profile → Edit Profile → remove or replace.

For deletion of any other specific data, email shabkda@gmail.com and we will action it within 30 days.

If you have questions about deletion or data retention, see our Privacy Policy or contact us at shabkda@gmail.com.
